iPhone (Apple Calendar)

  1. Tap Open in Calendar above on your iPhone, or
  2. Settings → Calendar → Accounts → Add Account → Other → Add Subscribed Calendar, paste the URL.

Android / Google Calendar

  1. Open calendar.google.com on a desktop browser.
  2. Other calendars → From URL, paste the URL above, click Add calendar.
  3. Open the Google Calendar app on your phone — the fixtures appear there automatically.

Outlook

  1. Outlook on the web → Calendar → Add calendar → Subscribe from web, paste the URL.
